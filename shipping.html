Shipping Policy — R.U.T.13

Shipping

We calculate shipping charges on a per-product basis. Orders are shipped FOB Matthews, NC. Product, shipping, and handling charges will be charged at time of shipment. Shipping charges are non-refundable.

Rut13.com understands that getting your items quickly is important to you, so we make every effort to process your order quickly. We send orders out within 1 to 2 business days of your purchase date excluding Major Holidays. When your order is received, we will send you an email confirming your order.

One shipping address per order is required. If more ‘ship to’ addresses are needed, please place an additional order for each location.

When you order from our website, you can expect to receive your order within 3–7 business days of the shipping date. We use UPS, FedEx Ground, and the United States Postal Service (USPS) Priority Service for all domestic shipments to the contiguous United States (the 48 American states on the continent of North America that are south of Canada and north of Mexico, plus the District of Columbia. The term excludes the states of Alaska and Hawaii, and all off-shore U.S. territories and possessions, such as Puerto Rico).

All PO Box addresses must be shipped via USPS. UPS’ delivery time is 3-7 days depending on your location in the country. International shipments are sent via the USPS and delivery time will vary depending on factors such as your local postal service, and the customs practices of your country.

R.U.T. 13 is not liable for any lost shipments when USPS is selected as the shipping method. The consumer is responsible for claiming all misplaced, lost or undelivered shipments directly with USPS.

If you would like to ship on an account or require additional information, please contact us by phone at 803.804.7487 or by email at info@rut13.com.
