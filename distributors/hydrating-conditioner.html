Daily Hydrating Conditioner Natural and Organic

Daily moisturizing conditioner for normal, color treated and dehydrated hair. Formulated with Organic Shea Butter.

Daily Hydrating Conditioner

This deep hydration conditioner is extremely effective for damaged, dry, dull, or chemically treated hair. Infuses deep conditioning oils, vitamins, and antioxidants to leave hair manageable, frizz-free, and with radiant sheen. Combined with Fractionated Coconut Oil and natural extracts of Borage Seed Oil providing one of the highest concentrations of essential fatty acids, restoring smoothness and moisture and Mistletoe promoting strength and shine.

Directions
  • Apply to wet hair, let penetrate for up to five minutes.
  • Rinse well.
  • Use with R.U.T.13 Daily Hydrating Shampoo and R.U.T.13 Pre-Styling Tonic.
Ingredients
Key Ingredients:
Mistletoe, Borage Seed Oil and Fractionated Coconut Oil
