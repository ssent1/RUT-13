Leave-In Wearable Treatment Natural and Organic

Calms frizz, moisturizes and prevents split ends. Works well with thick or fine hair types, hair frequently under a blow dryer or flat iron. Weightless formula leaves hair smooth, silky and bouncy.

Leave-In Wearable Treatment

An amazing all-in-one product. R.U.T. 13 Leave-In Wearable Treatment is a weightless formulation complimented with Fractionated Coconut Oil and Aloe Vera Oil improving hydration, encouraging cell rejuvenation and Golden Seal adding shine and helping to seal in moisture.

  • Use anytime to boost moisture.
