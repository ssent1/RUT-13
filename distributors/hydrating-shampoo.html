Daily Hydrating Shampoo - R.U.T.13 Organic Hair Therapy

Daily Hydrating Shampoo Natural and Organic

Daily Moisturizing Shampoo for normal, color treated and dehydrated hair. Formulated with Organic Shea Butter.

Daily Hydrating Shampoo

This ultra-hydrating shampoo moisturizes dry, stressed, thick or coarse hair. Formulated with silk and wheat proteins to rejuvenate and rebuild damaged hair with Fractionated Coconut Oil and naturally derived extracts of Borage Seed Oil providing one of the highest concentrations of essential fatty acids, restoring smoothness and moisture combined with Mistletoe promoting strength and shine.

Directions
  • Apply a small amount to wet hair.
  • Lather and rinse.
  • Reapply, lather and rinse again.
  • Condition with R.U.T.13 Daily Hydrating Conditioner and R.U.T.13 Pre-Styling Tonic.
Ingredients
Key Ingredients:
Mistletoe, Borage Seed Oil, and Coconut Oil

Aqua (Purified Water), Sodium Lauryl Sulfoacetate, Disodium Laureth Sulfosuccinate, Lauryl Glucoside, Decyl Glucoside, Cocamidopropyl Hydroxysultaine, Cocamidopropylamine Oxide, Polyquaternium-28, Glycerine, Organic Butyrospermum Parkii (Shea Nut Butter), Borage Seed Oil, Mistletoe, Fractionated Coconut Oil, Guar Hydroxypropyltrimonium Chloride, Silk Protein, Wheat Protein, Polyquaternium-7, Propylene Glycol, Iodopropynyl Butylcarbamate, Diazolidinyl Urea, Polyquaternium-28, Parfum, Citric Acid
